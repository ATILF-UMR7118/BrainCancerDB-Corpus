# BrainCancerDB-Corpus

📜This is the github page for the BioNLP 2026 paper **Evaluating LLM-as-a-Judge for Medical Term Simplification**

🔗 https://aclanthology.org/2026.bionlp-1.55/

📍Presented as a poster at ACL 2026, in San Diego, California, US

Poster Session Balboa A-B - Friday, July 3 at 15:30

**TLDR**: Our research shows that while LLM-Judges are similar to humans in evaluating text simplification, they are lenient when it comes to medical correctness.

Authors: Ioana Buhnila, Aman Sinha, Rohit Agarwal, Dilip Prasad, Mathieu Constant.
